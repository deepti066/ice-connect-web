About Us

Ice Connect ISP Private Limited is involved in constructing the next-generation internet services for enterprise clients in Assam. We provide secured fiber optic broadband and Internet Lease Line (ILL) services to enterprise clients in Assam. Ice Connect ISP Private Limited holds an all-Assam Class-B ISP license issued by the Department of Telecommunications (DoT), Govt. of India.

Ice Connect ISP Private Limited is also developing a full-service framework capable of providing leading-edge voice, video, IT applications, and multimedia content services over any broadband or IP-based network. This framework can be replicated by us for internet service providers around the world. We partner with top technology leaders to ensure the best solutions.
