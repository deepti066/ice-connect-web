About Us

Ice Connect ISP Private Limited leads the development of next-generation enterprise internet services in Assam. We are the experts in deploying secure fiber broadband and Internet Lease Line (ILL) services as per the specific requirements of local businesses. Ice Connect ISP Private Limited possesses all-Assam Class-B ISP approval, issued by the Department of Telecommunications (DoT), Government of India.

Apart from our core services, Ice Connect ISP Private Limited is working actively on a complete service framework that facilitates the delivery of next-generation voice, video, IT applications, and multimedia content over any broadband or IP-based network. This state-of-the-art framework is made replicable for internet service providers worldwide. We work with top technology partners to provide the best quality solutions to our customers.

Our core competency is in providing Broadband and Internet Lease Line (ILL) services

BroadBand Services

Our high-speed Broadband services ensure reliable and efficient internet connectivity for businesses of all sizes. Designed to support various applications, from everyday browsing to cloud operations, we offer scalable solutions to meet your specific needs.

Internet Lease Line (ILL) Services

Our Internet Lease Line (ILL) provides dedicated, uninterrupted connectivity, ensuring consistent speed and reliability for critical business applications. Ideal for VoIP, cloud computing, and large data transfers, our ILL services come with customizable bandwidth.
